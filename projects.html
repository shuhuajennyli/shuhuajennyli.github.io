STEM Sorority

Empowering girls and gender minorities to pursue a career in STEM and overcome gender obstacles in the field through unique science activities, webinars with top scientists, and Project Escape, an innovative science-themed escape room that has captivated the imagination of hundreds of elementary school students across Greater Vancouver.

Chessle

A Wordle spinoff for chess enthusiasts peaking at 25,000 daily users.

Full stack development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to perform a variety of tasks in a team of two from bug fixes/small improvements to introducing Piece Square Matching notation and a new mode of gameplay.

Nanocellulose- and Modified Wood -Based Sorbents for Oily Waste Cleanup

Co-authored book chapter published by world leading publisher CRC Press, Taylor & Francis Group. In collaboration with Dr. Feng Jiang and Dr. Yeling Zhu of the University of British Columbia Sustainable Functional Biomaterials Lab.

Deep-Learning Based Heartbeat Analysis for the Detection of Cardiac Arrhythmia

CNN that analyzes an ECG recording of a heartbeat for the classification of healthy cases and four types of cardiac arrhythmia. The final model had varying accuracies for each of the diseases, with 97% of unhealthy cases classified as unhealthy and 98% of healthy cases classified as healthy.
